只需设置permalink即可：
```yml
---
author: xaoxuu
avatar: https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/xaoxuu/assets@master/avatar/avatar.png
author_url: https://xaoxuu.com
permalink: https://xaoxuu.com/wiki/material-x/
---
```
项目分类中的均为外部文章，[前往查看](/projects/)。
